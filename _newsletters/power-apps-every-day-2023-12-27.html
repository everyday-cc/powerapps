{% raw %}Create relationships, business rules, calculations, and rollups in Common Data Service{% endraw %} Do you need to create data relationships, business rules, calculations, and rollups in Common Data Service? These modules help you use Common Data Service to build powerful business solutions that will transform your operations, processes, and your entire organization. The learning path Get started with Common Data Service introduces you to Common Data Service and many of the key concepts which include environment, entities, fields, and options sets. This learning path continues and expands the exploration of Common Data Service with an overview how to form relationships between entities, how to build business rules to perform logic based upon your organization's needs, and how to add calculations and rollup fields.
